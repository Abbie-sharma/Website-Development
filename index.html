About The Conference


About The Conference

The purpose of the AIST-2019 conference is to promote research in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Speech Technology (ST) and foster scientific exchange between researchers, practitioners, scientists, students, and engineers in Artificial Intelligence, Speech Technology and its affiliated disciplines. It will involve a number of reasearch activities like technical paper presentations, invited speakers, tutorials, all selected according to the highest standards. AIST-2019 will also include additional programs for students and young researchers.
Two Best Papers will be awarded. Selected papers will be published in SCOPUS/Taylor and Francis/Springer Journals.

Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW) has been upgraded from Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology in May 2013 vide Delhi State Legislature Act 9, 2012, as a non-affiliating teaching and research University at Delhi to facilitate and promote studies, research, technology, innovation, incubation and extension work in emerging areas of professional education among women, with focus on engineering, technology, applied sciences, management and its allied areas with the objective to achieve excellence in these and related fields.
