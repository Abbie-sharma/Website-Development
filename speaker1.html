Speaker Details

Professor Nakamura

At present, Nakamura is Professor, Department of Information and Communication Systems, Faculty of Systems Engineering, Wakayama University

Some of his Academic Activities are:-

Full member of the Robotics Society of Japan, Editorial Committee member of the Robotics Society of Japan (1998-1999)

Kitano Symbiotic System Project Research Promotion Committee (1999-2001)

Japan Society for Robotics Paper Award and Research Encouragement Award Selection Committee Member (2000)

Director of Non-profit Specified Corporation RoboCup Japan Committee (1999-2001)

International Conference of The Society for Adaptive Behavior 1998 Program Committee

International Conference of The Society for Adaptive Behavior 2000 Program Committee

Program committee member of the 20th Annual Conference of the Robotics Society of Japan (2002)

RoboCup2002 middle-size league local organizing committee

9th Intelligent Mechatronics Workshop Executive Committee Member (2004)

Editorial Committee Member, System Control Information Society

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Speech Technology
